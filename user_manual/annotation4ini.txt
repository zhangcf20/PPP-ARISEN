[annotation for ARISEN.ini]
After_OT：expanded epoch number after the origin time of seismology(int)
BDS_OBS_TYPE：priority of BDS observations(";" is for default priority)
Batch：batch process or not(options: true/false)
Before_OT：expanded epoch number before the origin time of seismology(int)
CSI_ZWDV：inflection point of ZWD Variance(int)
Cut_angle：cutoff angle of observation satellites(double)
DeleteSats：delete specified satellites(example："G01;G02")
Elevation_weight_function：different trigonometric function models(options: GAMIT/CNES/Bernese/PANDA)
Filter_direction：forward or backsmooth(options：Forward/Backsmooth)
GLONASS_OBS_TYPE：priority of GLONASS observations(";" is for default priority)
GPS_OBS_TYPE：priority of GPS observations(";" is for default priority)
Galileo_OBS_TYPE：priority of Galileo observations(";" is for default priority)
Initialize：how to initialize PPP(options：SPP/SNX/CSV)
Interval_original：original interval of observation(int)
Interval_used：interval of observation in used(int)
Kin_model：kinematic noise model(options：Whitenoise/Randomwalk）
LP_precision：precision of L and P("0.003;3.0" is default (unit: m))
OTh：the origin hour of seismology(int)
OTm：the origin minute of seismology(int)
OTs：the origin secend of seismology(int)
PPP_model：model of PPP(options：Static/Kinematic/Seismologic)
Path_ini：file path of initialization(for initialization of SNX/CSV)
Path_save：file path to save the status of the last filter epoch(if you do not want to save it, just keep is empty!)
Pk：filter variance in Kalman of initialization, there are 5 parameters in succession of pos,zwd,clk,amb,ion like: "-1;-1;-1;-1;-1"(-1 means default)
Qw：model variance in Kalman filter, there are 5 parameters in succession of pos,zwd,clk,amb,ion like: "-1;-1;-1;-1;-1"(-1 means default)
Randomwalk：the kinematic noise of Randomwalk(double) and it only works in randomwalk mode (unit: cm/s)
Sampling：change the sampling or not(options: true/false)
Satellites_system：the sat system in used(G: GPS; R: GLONASS; C: BDS; E: Galileo; example：GE)
Startup_interface: startup interface or not(options: true/false)
Strategy：strategy to deal with observation(options: IF/SSD(IF)/ARC(SSD)/ARF(SSD)
Trop_map：mapping function of troposphere(options：GMF/NMF/VMF1/VMF3)
Trop_model：troposphere model(options：UNB3m/Sasstam(GPT2)/Hopfield(GPT2)/Sasstam(GPT3))
Threshold_for_o_file_size(Mb)：threshold for observing file size(int)
Weight_GNSS：Weight between GNSS(G:R:C:E)(double)
Productdir：file path of products
Resultdir：file path of output
Rinexdir：file path of observation
